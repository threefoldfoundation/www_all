# Digital Twin HyperDrive

Digital twin supports https://hypercore-protocol.org/ which is an amazing [Peer2Peer](threefold__peer2peer) protocol to share information between digital twins and users. Digital

Any information on the [dtfs])(dtfs) can be synced to the Digital Twin Hyperdrive.

## implementation

The backend of the digital twin hypedrive is stored on a [dtfs](threefold__dtfs.md) as well, which means the data can never be lost.
