ThreeFold @ Odyssey Momentum 2020!

Momentum is an online event of 49 hours with more than 2,000 participants and 105 teams working on 21 complex 21st-century problems.

Sam Taggart

13 November 2020

This weekend, ThreeFold will be involved in Odyssey Momentum 2020 – Momentum is an online event of 49 hours with more than 2,000 participants and 105 teams working on 21 complex 21st-century problems.

ThreeFold is one of the platforms which can be used by participants, and we also have a ThreeFold team member there as a "jedi," who will be supporting participants in developing their solutions.

May the force be with all participants this weekend! 🙏
